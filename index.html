⋆.˚ Deforestation on Biodiversity ⋆.˚

Biodiversity is basically in short is all the life that exist on earth today. Deforestation is the most common loss of biodiversity. Forests are cleared because of many reasons but mainly because of urbanizaton Urbanizaton causes the environment to lose its ability to sustain life. Our website aims to raise awarness about the harmful affects of cutting down trees and harming the environment. We offers ways for indivituals to contribute to this. This could be from anything. For eg: Creating awareness, Providing donations or even something as simple as creating sustainable choices. This website is a step closer towards protecting our environment as we know it for our future generations.
